Our Mission

Silent Santa connects generous donors with children in need during the holiday season. We believe every child deserves to experience the magic of receiving a special gift that's just for them.

Through our platform, school administrators can submit letters from children, and kind-hearted donors can fulfill these wishes directly. It's a simple concept with a powerful impact - bringing joy to children while giving donors a meaningful way to give back.

We handle the logistics, you provide the magic!

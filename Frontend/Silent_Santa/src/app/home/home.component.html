How It Works

1

Letters Posted

School administrators post heartfelt letters from children in need, detailing their Christmas wishes.

2

Donors Choose

Generous donors browse letters and select children they wish to help by preparing gift packages.

3

Gifts Prepared

Donors prepare gifts based on the child's wishes and mark items as completed in their checklist.

4

Joy Delivered

Once ready, gifts are delivered to the children, bringing joy and fulfilling their Christmas wishes.
